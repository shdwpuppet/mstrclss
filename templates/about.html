About Master of Classes

This event came to be one morning a few weeks ago when BUICK, samiface, ScottJAw, and KevinIsPwn were hanging out in a mumble casually talking about ways they could benefit the community. After about an hour of conversation, the ideas for the "Masters of Classes" event—a name Kevin is a little bit too proud of—really started developing into an actual event. A few weeks and many man hours later, here we are!

"Masters of Classes" is an event run under TF2Stadium, but there are several people both involved with and not involved with TF2Stadium that were pivotal in the creation of this event.

KevinIsPwn

Runs newbie mixes, helps with tf2stadium, and runs various tournaments for tf2. Kevin orchestrated a lot of the organizational structure and behind the scenes stuff for the event. He also created some of the course images.

Dr.shdwpuppet

Helps out in various roles behind the scenes in a few tf2 orgs. He created the backend code for this website and helped in organizing the event as a whole.

Samiface

Was an event coordinator for Pheonix Comicon for four years. She recently joined the tf2 community as a competitive player and streamer. She helped with a lot of the behind the scenes nitty-gritty.

Capu

Is the frontend developer and graphic designer for TF2Stadium. He created the shell of this website which Dr.shdwpuppet has branched off of.

ScottJAw

Is a TF2 youtuber. He helped largely in the brainstorming phases.

Randal

Is a newbie mix admin. He is the head of the individual course-admins and also helped organize the event.

BUICK

Is a TF2 caster and personality. He helped organize some classes along with some of the partnerships MoC is using.
